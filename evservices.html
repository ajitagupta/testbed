EV Services FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our electric vehicle services.

How do I reserve a charging station for my stay? +

Charging is automatically included with your reservation. During booking, you'll be asked about your vehicle type and charging preferences. Our system will reserve appropriate charging time slots based on your stay duration and needs.

What if all charging stations are in use when I need to charge? +

Our smart scheduling system prevents this issue by allocating charging time slots based on guest needs. In rare peak situations, our concierge will ensure you get access to charging within your requested timeframe or offer alternative solutions.

Is there an additional cost for charging my vehicle? +

Standard Level 2 charging is included at no additional cost up to 50 kWh per day. DC fast charging incurs a nominal fee to cover higher electricity costs. Exact rates are displayed during booking and on our mobile app.
