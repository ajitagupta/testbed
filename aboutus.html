ElectriStay's Vision

Founded by a team of passionate EV enthusiasts and tech entrepreneurs, our journey began with a shared vision to create a haven where travel and sustainability meet. ElectriStay is more than accommodation—it's a movement towards responsible, electrified travel.

Our Mission

Elevating EV Travel Experiences

We are dedicated to curating extraordinary experiences that inspire, rejuvenate, and connect our guests with the excitement of sustainable travel. Every property in our network is carefully selected to provide seamless charging solutions while offering exceptional comfort and luxury.

Exceeding Expectations

From personalized concierge services to attentive hospitality, we are committed to surpassing every expectation. Our team ensures that every aspect of your stay is flawless, from the moment you plug in your vehicle to the moment you check out refreshed and recharged.

Creating Unforgettable Memories

We believe that the journey is as important as the destination. By combining premium accommodations with state-of-the-art EV infrastructure, we create unforgettable travel experiences that demonstrate how luxury and sustainability can go hand in hand.
