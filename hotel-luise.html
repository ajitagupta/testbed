About Hotel Luise

Hotel Luise is a charming boutique hotel located in the heart of Erlangen, Germany. Combining traditional Bavarian hospitality with modern amenities, this eco-friendly establishment has been a pioneer in sustainable tourism in the region for over 15 years. The hotel underwent a significant renovation in 2020 to integrate state-of-the-art EV charging facilities, making it one of the premier destinations for electric vehicle travelers in Bavaria.

The 45-room property offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience, with elegantly designed rooms featuring contemporary furnishings, premium bedding, and smart room controls. Each room is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, smart TVs with streaming capabilities, and ergonomic workspaces for business travelers.

Hotel Luise is particularly proud of its commitment to sustainability. The property runs on 100% renewable energy, with solar panels providing a significant portion of its electricity needs. The hotel's restaurant, Gr√ºner Tisch, specializes in farm-to-table Bavarian cuisine, sourcing ingredients from local organic farms within a 50-kilometer radius.

Located just 5 minutes from Erlangen's historic market square and 10 minutes from Friedrich-Alexander University, Hotel Luise offers the perfect base for exploring the charming university town and the broader Nuremberg metropolitan area.
