About Hotel Luise

The Hotel Luise is a unique city hotel in the heart of Erlangen. As a third-generation family business, it combines Franconian hospitality with innovative sustainability - and has been doing so for for several decades.

With its climate-positive concept, the hotel is one of the pioneers of sustainable tourism in Germany.

The hotel offers 92 individually designed rooms, including Circular Hotel Rooms whose components are either 100% biodegradable or 100% recyclable. Guests can look forward to amenities such as a spa, a green roof terrace and special conference facilities.

Sustainability is practiced holistically at Hotel Luise: the hotel has implemented over 270 sustainability measures, and 100% of its electricity comes from renewable energy sources, while its own solar system provides additional energy. Those arriving by electric car can charge it directly at the hotel. Breakfast also follows the green concept: regional, seasonal and prepared with love - from Franconian jams to organic coffee.

Thanks to its central location, the Hotel Luise is the perfect starting point for discovering Erlangen - be it the charming old town, the Friedrich Alexander University or the vibrant cultural and gastronomic offerings. Experience a stay that is not only relaxing but also inspiring.
